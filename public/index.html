Michael Stallings' ToDo-List-API

A very simple daily to-do app. Enter the time of your todo in 4 digit 24-hr time (ex: 1PM = 1300)
Then enter the task you want to do at that time, click the blue submit button and voila! Your todo is added to today's running list.
In this first-version, you will have to click submit on an empty time and empty todo box to load the last created todo into the list.
Work on newer versions that are more intuitive are underway.
